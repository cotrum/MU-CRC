About the CRC

At Monmouth University’s Cybersecurity Research Center (CRC), we engage students and faculty in real-world digital defence, innovative research, and community outreach. We provide tools, platforms, and collaborative opportunities to prepare tomorrow’s cyber defenders — today.

Research & Initiatives

Adversarial Network Simulation

Developing simulated attack/defense environments to evaluate resilience of modern networks and IoT infrastructures.

Augmented Reality CTF Platform

An immersive AR-based CTF framework to teach cybersecurity through gameplay, real scenarios, and in-field challenges.

Data Privacy & Crypto Systems

Advancing cryptographic techniques and privacy-preserving architectures for next-gen information systems.
